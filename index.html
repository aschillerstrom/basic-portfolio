About Me

I am a web designer based in Austin, Texas. My approach to design is to be simplistic yet cinematically inspired. I love the creative process in designing websites due to the never-ending possibilities.

I was introduced to coding when I was 7 years old and have coded as a hobby ever since. I am currently a student at the University of Texas Coding Bootcamp to solidify my skills.
